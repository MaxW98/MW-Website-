The Cloud


Cloud computing is a general term for the delivery of hosted services over the internet

Cloud Computing allows companies as well as general consumers to gather and upload resources; storage, virtual machines and aplications can be maintained on this utility. Rather than having to maintain and build a computing infraturcture within your home cloud computing does this for you at a remote location. Allowing your data to be stored in another location boasts many benefits including; security, increase of space and easy access.

Cloud computing, often referred to as simply “the cloud,” is the delivery of on-demand computing resources - everything from applications to data centers - over the internet on a pay - for - use basis.

  • Elastic resources - Scale up or down quickly and easily to meet demand
  • Metered service so you only pay for what you use
  • Self service - All the IT resources you need with self-service access
